    A Fusion of Culture

    This concert takes you through a carefully curated program of masterworks from the Western Classical and Carnatic, (an Indian Classical art form) musical Canons but also Evergreen hits from the Bollywood Film Industry. The program has been created with variety and exploration at it's core, and to celebrate not just one culture's music, but the tradition of the art form as a whole.
