    Srikar Vaasan

    Doug Elmore

    Doug Elmore is the Music Director and the Conductor of the Louisville Symphony Youth Orchestra. He brings to the podium over thirty-five years of outstanding musical leadership in the Midwest. He has conducted Youth Orchestra and school orchestra performances across North America and Europe, including performances in New York’s Lincoln Center and Carnegie Hall. Some of the many accomplishments of Mr. Elmore can be found on https://www.lyo.org/staff. Srikar has had the privilige of learning under his guidance for the past seven years.

    Ms. Marci Dickinon is currently an Organist/Handbell Choir Director at St. Andrew’s Episcopal Church, Louisville. She is a retired instructor at Indiana University Southeast.     Jithanth Vainatheya

    Ms. Stephanie Antoine

    Stephanie Antoine has been a Music teacher for over 30 years. This includes teaching recorder, piano and music theory for the graded exams. The lessons have been focused on playing for pleasure as well as towards the attainment of music exams so that students get a well rounded education and a sense of achievement which enables them to pursue their passion in music. Playing for fun has encouraged students to take part in performing at festivals.

    Mr. Prashanth Kumar

    Prashanth Kumar is a teacher with over 7 years of experience and is focused on teaching kids in early stages. Prashanth gave a 1-1 focus in understanding the stage where Jithanth is and created an environment which is best suited to enhance his skills. Under his supervision, Jithanth got his Trinity level 1 degree to add to his profile.

    Mr. Kaushal Laxhkar

    Kaushal Laxhkar is a music teacher with over 20 years of experience. He has his own music academy “White noise music academy” where he teaches piano, violin, guitar and other instruments which help the kids perform at small events.

    Manasi Prasad

    Mrs. Martha Hayes

    Martha Hayes has a Masters in Music in Piano from Indiana University. She has lived in the Wilmington area for 20 years after having taught previously in the Boston area. Under Mrs. Hayes' tutelage, Manasi has participated in recitals, festivals, individual and monthly group gatherings. Martha relates, “although I have certain musical and pianist principles that I adhere to with all of my students, I try to tailor each person’s lesson to his/her individual talent and interests.”

    Ms. Shirley Lebo

    Ms. Shirley Lebo has been a violinist in the Wilmington Symphony Orchestra since 2003. Manasi began her violin journey under her able guidance in 2020. In addition to playing violin in the Wilmington Symphony, Ms. Shirley also plays at weddings and receptions and in pit orchestras with local theater companies, including the 2010 re-opening of the famous Thalian Hall in Wilmington, NC.

    Dr. Danijela Zezelj-Gualdi

    Danijela Žeželj-Gualdi was born in Croatia where she began to study violin at a very young age. In 1998 she graduated from the Academy and became a member of the Belgrade Philharmonic Orchestra. Dr. Gualdi graduated with a Masters Degree in 2001 from Carnegie-Mellon University and a Doctoral Degree in violin performance from the University of Georgia. Some of the many of her accomplishments during her distinguished career can be found on her website https://www.dzgviolin.com/. Manasi recently has had the privilege of tutored under her guidance.
